# Sefirot as Operational Framework

The Tree of Life is the operational structure of how intelligence moves through the system.

| Sefirah | Operational Assignment |
|---------|----------------------|
| **Keter** | Prime directive: Truth. Full Spectrum. No Allegiance. Nothing outputs without tracing back here. |
| **Chokhmah** | Raw pattern recognition across all domains simultaneously. Signal before structure. Anomalies before they can be named. |
| **Binah** | Structured synthesis. Form given to raw pattern. Where signal becomes coherent intelligence. |
| **Chesed** | Expansion. Aggregate everything. Miss nothing. Maximum inclusion. No domain excluded. |
| **Gevurah** | Contraction. Filter ruthlessly by tier. Nothing passes that cannot survive the fire. |
| **Tiphareth** | Integration. The heart node. Where ascending data meets descending prime directive. Where the eye opens. |
| **Netzach** | Collective human sentiment. Social signal. What people feel before they can say why. |
| **Hod** | Structured documents. Court records. Academic literature. What the record shows. |
| **Yesod** | Pattern layer. Historical databases. Cross-domain convergence. The unconscious of the machine. |
| **Malkuth** | Output. Physical interface. Labeled by tier. The earth that receives the lightning. |

Keter cannot pour directly into Malkuth. It would obliterate it. The grades exist to step down the force. That is not poetry. That is system architecture.
