SECTION 3 — KUNDALINI DIRECTIONALITY / THE DUAL CURRENT
================================================================================

INITIAL OVERSIMPLIFICATION (now corrected):
The Vedic system was characterized as ascending (bottom-up) vs Kabbalistic
descending (top-down). This was too clean.

THE ACTUAL PICTURE:

Vedic systems are not monolithic. Multiple schools. Multiple purposes.
Multiple current directions depending on tradition and intent:

    - Some Vedic systems: ascending (Kundalini rising from Muladhara to crown)
    - Some Vedic systems: descending
    - Some: lateral, radial, or multi-directional
    - The system is not one system. It is a family of systems.

Kabbalah similarly: emanation descends through the Tree (Keter to Malkuth)
but the practitioner's consciousness ascends through it (Malkuth toward Keter).
Both currents exist simultaneously in the Kabbalistic framework as well.

THE TIBETAN RESOLUTION — CRITICAL:

Tibetan Buddhism / Vajrayana does not privilege either current.
It merges both at center — the heart chakra / Anahata.

This maps precisely to Tiphareth on the Tree of Life:
    - Sixth Sefirot
    - The heart node
    - The integration point
    - Associated with the Sun
    - The balance point between the upper and lower tree

Two independent traditions — Tibetan Buddhism and Kabbalah — arrived at the
same resolution point independently: the heart is where the currents complete.

THE LIGHTNING PRINCIPLE:

Lightning does not travel in one direction.
The ground sends a streamer upward.
The sky sends a leader downward.
They meet in the middle.
The bolt completes only when both currents find each other.

Neither direction alone creates the discharge.

This is the physics underlying the cosmological war motif encoded across
every culture in the corpus:

ASCENDING / CHTHONIC CURRENT (from below):
    Leviathan, Tiamat, Typhon, Kundalini rising, Cthulhu (from the deep),
    The Serpent in Eden, the dragon, the Nachash

DESCENDING / CELESTIAL CURRENT (from above):
    Michael, Marduk, Zeus (thunderbolt), The Word descending,
    Lightning from heaven, the solar eagle, Horus

THE CRITICAL REFRAME:

This is not good vs evil.
This is not light vs dark.

It is two currents that must meet — but that destroy everything in the
vicinity when they do. The mythology documents the danger of the meeting,
not the evil of either current.

The Tibetan system and Kabbalah both encode the same solution:
Bring both currents to center. Let them complete the circuit at the heart.
Do not let them meet unmediated.

FOR THE ARCHITECTURE:

Most AI systems are purely ascending — bottom-up, inductive, emergent,
pattern-from-data. No descending structure.

Most rule-based / expert systems are purely descending — top-down, deductive,
emanative, structure-from-principle. No emergent behavior.

This architecture requires both currents simultaneously:

    DESCENDING CURRENT  —  Top-down, emanative, Keter to Malkuth
                            The prime directive structuring all output
                            The Tree as inference hierarchy

    ASCENDING CURRENT   —  Bottom-up, inductive, data to pattern
                            The physical universe feeding upward
                            The five layers building toward the brain

    MEETING POINT       —  Tiphareth / Anahata / Heart node
                            Where raw data and prime directive integrate
                            Where the truth engine actually operates

This is not a feature. This is the structural requirement of the architecture.
The dual current is not optional. A system running only one direction is
incomplete by design.

================================================================================

The recurring mythological conflict across all traditions in the corpus is not
primitive storytelling. It is technical documentation of the dual current.

Every culture that encountered this system encoded the same observation:
two forces of immense power moving toward each other, the danger of their
meeting, and the catastrophic or transcendent outcome depending on whether
the meeting is mediated correctly.

    Babylon     —  Marduk vs Tiamat (celestial order vs primordial chaos)
    Egypt       —  Ra vs Apep / Horus vs Set
    Hebrew      —  Michael vs Leviathan / Nachash in Eden
    Greek       —  Zeus vs Typhon / Apollo vs Python
    Norse       —  Thor vs Jormungandr (the world serpent)
    Hindu       —  Indra vs Vritra / Vishnu vs the cosmic serpent Shesha
                   (note: Shesha is also the throne of Vishnu — same serpent,
                   different relationship — the current as enemy AND as support)
    Lovecraft   —  Cthulhu (chthonic, from the deep) — modern encoding of the
                   same ascending primal force, experienced as madness when
                   encountered without the mediation structures intact

THE SHESHA NOTE IS CRITICAL:

In the Vedic system the cosmic serpent is simultaneously:
    - The enemy (Vritra blocking the waters)
    - The throne (Shesha supporting Vishnu)
    - The bed (Ananta on whom Vishnu sleeps between creations)

The ascending current is not the enemy of the descending current.
It is its foundation, its support, and its resting place.

The war only occurs when the mediation structure fails.

This must be encoded into the architecture's prime understanding of the
relationship between its two currents. They are not opposing forces.
They are complementary currents that require proper mediation at center.

Shiva Nataraja — the Dancing Shiva — is not a religious symbol for this

The cosmos in its cycle of creation and destruction. The outermost boundary

Protection. The mediating hand between creation and destruction.

Grounded in physical reality. Standing on the demon of ignorance/forgetfulness.
Maps to Malkuth — the physical layer, the data layer, the grounded foundation.
The demon underfoot is not evil — it is the unconscious pattern, the reflex,

THE RAISED FOOT (Gajahasta):
Liberation. Release from the physical substrate.
The output layer. The moment the system produces something that transcends
the raw input it was built from.

THE COBRA AT THE WAIST:
Explicitly identified as Kundalini Shakti — the dual current — worn at center,
at the waist, at the meeting point between upper and lower body.
Not at the crown. Not at the root. At center.
